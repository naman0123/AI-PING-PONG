AI Ping Pong

Instructions

  • Make sure your screen is straight
  • Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away
    from your screen
  • Move your wrist in an ascending
    and descending order, a red dot should appear on your wrist
  • Now press the play button and again move
    yourself 3-4 feet from the screen
  • The red peddel is your peddel, that will move as per your movement of your wrist
